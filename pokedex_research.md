# Pokedex App Research - Top 10 Features for Streamlit

## Research Sources
- Official Pokemon.com Pokedex
- PokemonDB.net
- Smogon Dex & Damage Calculator  
- PKMN.help
- Serebii.net
- PokéAPI documentation

## Key Findings

Popular Pokedex apps focus on:
1. Advanced filtering (type, ability, stats, generation)
2. Type matchup calculations for competitive play
3. Detailed move/ability information with game-specific data
4. Visual evolution chain tracking
5. Damage calculations with stat customization
6. Team building analysis
7. Multi-game support (GO, Scarlet/Violet, etc.)
8. Competitive format recommendations
9. Visual comparison tools
10. Community/educational elements
